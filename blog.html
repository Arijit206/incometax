Know your Income Tax

North East Region (NER) of India covers 7 states spread over a large geographical area (2.55 lakh sq.km), which is poorly connected and sparsely populated (176 persons per sq.km, against all-India average of 382 persons per sq.km). While India has 2.4% of the world surface area and 17.5% of the world’s population, NER has 7.8% of India’s surface area and 3.7% of India’s population.
